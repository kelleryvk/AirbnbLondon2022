Choosing the accommodation type

Deciding in which neighbourhood we would like to stay in is an important aspect of our trip to London.
Nevertheless, we might also be interested in picking an accommodation that is suitable to our needs.

Entire home/apartment

Entire home/apartments are definitely the most popular in neighbourhoods like Westminster, Camden and Kensington & Chelsea.
However, as we have discovered in the previous bubble charts, they tend to be quite expensive compared to other neighbourhoods.
If we are on a budget but we also value privacy, we might want to opt for Islington, Tower Hamlets or Hackney.

Private room

Private rooms are also quite popular among hosts on AirBnB.
Especially in neighbourhood that tend to be slightly cheaper like Tower Hamlets, Hackney but also Southwark.
We also have to consider there will be a substantial difference in price between booking an entire home/apt or getting a private room.

Shared room

Shared rooms are more difficult to find in London. Nevertheless, it seems Westminster, Camden and Kensington & Chelsea dominate even in these types of offerings.

Hotel room

Similar to shared rooms, hotel rooms are also more difficult to find in London.
Nevertheless, it seems Westminster, Camden and Kensington & Chelsea dominate even in these types of offerings.
If we decide to book a hotel room via AirBnB, we might want to review our choice of selected neighbourhoods, as only a few central areas seem to have listings.
